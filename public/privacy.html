GotaGuy ("we," "us," or "our") operates the gotaguymckinney.com website and the GotaGuy SMS-based home repair service. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

When you text GotaGuy or use our website, we may collect:

  • Your phone number

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Scope your repair job and provide a price estimate

  • Coordinate scheduling between you and the contractor

  • Send you SMS messages related to your service request, including confirmations, updates, and follow-ups

SMS Messaging

By texting GotaGuy at +1 (469) 273-6216, you consent to receive SMS messages from us related to your service request. These messages may include job scoping questions, price quotes, scheduling confirmations, contractor updates, and payment-related messages.

Message frequency varies based on your service request. Message and data rates may apply.

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. After opting out, you will receive no further messages from us. Reply HELP for assistance.

We share your information only as necessary to provide the service:

  • With contractors matched to your job (your name, address, job description, and scheduling details)
  • With Stripe for payment processing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our service and fulfill our legal obligations. You can request deletion of your data by texting us or emailing wade@kerzie.ai.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. All payment processing is handled by Stripe using industry-standard encryption.
